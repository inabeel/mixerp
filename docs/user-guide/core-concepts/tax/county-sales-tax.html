County Sales Taxes

In United States and some other countries, there are several federal taxes including the county sales tax. A sales tax can differ from one county to the other. Similarly, a county sales tax can have different rates based on:

  • Type of Customer (Establishment/Corporation/Entity)
  • Type of Customer (Industry)
  • Type of Item
  • Any combination of the above three.
